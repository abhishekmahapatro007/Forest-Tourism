Bra

Brazilian Forest Tour

Brazilian forests, particularly the Amazon rainforest, are renowned for their global significance. They are the world's largest tropical rainforest and home to unparalleled biodiversity, including iconic species like jaguars, toucans, and anacondas. These forests are vital for climate regulation, storing vast amounts of carbon and providing essential ecosystem services. However, they face threats from deforestation, illegal logging, and land encroachment.
